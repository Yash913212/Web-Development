Human-robot

In human-robot collatioration, the robot assists the human operator. This means: The machine does not replace the human, but complements his capab ities and relieves him of arduous tasks. These can include overhead work, for example, or the lifting of heavy loads. Autonomous. collaborative robots are also used to supply production workstations
